Honoring the Life and Legacy of Nelson Mandela

Nelson Mandela

About

Nelson Mandela was the first black President of South Africa and a global symbol of peace and justice. He was an anti-apartheid activist and politician who served as President from 1994 to 1999. Mandela was South Africa’s first black head of state and the first elected in a fully democratic election.

Born: 18 July 1918, Mvezo, South Africa
Died: 5 December 2013 (aged 95), Johannesburg, South Africa
Spouses: Graça Machel, Winnie Mandela, Evelyn Mase
Children: Zindziswa Mandela, Zenani Mandela
Awards: Nobel Peace Prize, Bharat Ratna, Nishan-e-Pakistan
Presidential Term: 10 May 1994 – 16 June 1999
Education: University of South Africa (1989).
