About Nairobi

Nairobi is the capital and largest city of Kenya. Known as the "Green City in the Sun," Nairobi offers a mix of modern city life and rich culture, wildlife, and nature.

Cultural Attractions

  • Nairobi National Museum – Showcases Kenya's rich heritage including history, nature, culture, and contemporary art.
  • Maasai Market – A vibrant open-air market offering handmade crafts, jewelry, and traditional fabrics from Kenyan artisans.
  • Bomas of Kenya – A cultural center where you can experience traditional dances, music, and architectural styles from Kenya’s various tribes.
  • Kenya National Theatre – A venue for performing arts including plays, music concerts, and cultural showcases that highlight Kenyan talent.

Popular Foods in Nairobi

Nairobi is a food hub with restaurants offering local dishes like:

  1. Nyama Choma – A Kenyan favorite, typically made with goat or beef, grilled over open flames and enjoyed with friends and family.
  2. Ugali – A staple food in Kenya, made from maize flour and water, often served as a side with meat, vegetables, or stew.
  3. Chapati – A soft, flaky flatbread of Indian origin, commonly eaten with lentils, stews, or tea.
  4. Mutura (Kenyan sausage) – A popular street food made from spiced meat and blood, traditionally grilled and served hot.
